I am a biomedical researcher by training.

I am studying functional and molecular implications of cancer-associated mutations in a critical tumor suppressor. Prior to this, I studied Adenovirus-induced cellular DNA damage response, cell cycle, and DNA replication. I have also investigated the efficacy of artemisinin- and lumefantrine-based combination therapy (ACT) in treating Plasmodium falciparum malaria in Nepal.
